OLIVER HAK
EVENTS MEDIA CONTACT

INTRODUCTION

Born in 2010, 15-year-old Polish pianist Oliver Hak has emerged as a multi-award-winning, internationally-recognised young artist. He made his solo piano recital debut at age 12 and has since performed in concerts and solo recitals across the UK, France, Switzerland, Poland, and Denmark.

His competition successes include:

  • First Prize and two Special Prizes at the 6th Concours International Piano Neuchâtel – Val de Travers (16–25 age category) in Switzerland
  • First Prize at the 25th International Piano Competition of Île-de-France in Paris
  • First Prize in the 2023 Junior Intercollegiate Piano Competition by the Beethoven Piano Society of Europe (BPSE) in London
  • First Prize at the 5th Scottish International Youth Piano Competition (Junior Division) in 2023
  • First Prize and the Sam Hutchings Memorial Award at the 7th Scottish International Youth Piano Competition (Senior Division) in 2025
  • Finalist in the 7th Aarhus International Piano Competition in Denmark

In the same year as his recital debut, he made his piano concerto debut with the RCS Junior Symphony Orchestra and later performed with the National Youth Orchestras of Scotland (NYOS), appearing in the Aberdeen Music Hall and Glasgow Royal Concert Hall. He was also featured in International Piano Magazine in September 2023.
