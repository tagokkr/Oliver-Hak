Introduction

Born in 2010, 15-year-old British-Polish pianist Oliver Hak has emerged as a multi-award-winning, internationally-recognised young artist. He made his solo piano recital debut at age 12 and has since performed in concerts and solo recitals across the UK, France, Switzerland, Poland, and Denmark.
